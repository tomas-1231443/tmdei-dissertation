\textit{
	[
		Example: "The application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) in SOCs has garnered significant attention as a potential solution to these challenges. 
		AI and ML algorithms can process vast amounts of data, identify patterns, and improve over time, potentially reducing the burden on human analysts. 
		Despite its promise, the deployment of AI in SOCs raises important questions about the reliability, transparency, and effectiveness of such systems in real-world environments. 
		Key theories of machine learning, supervised and unsupervised learning, and natural language processing (NLP) are central to the approach, alongside practical concerns around data quality, model adaptability, and false positive rates."
	]
